## How to Use These References

- **Starting a new deployment?** Begin with the [Copilot Admin Toggles](copilot-admin-toggles.md) and [License Requirements](license-requirements.md) to understand what you need.
- **Preparing for an examination?** Use the [Regulatory Mappings](regulatory-mappings.md) and [NIST AI RMF Crosswalk](nist-ai-rmf-crosswalk.md) to demonstrate coverage.
- **Configuring a specific portal?** Use the [Portal Paths Quick Reference](portal-paths-quick-reference.md) to navigate directly to the right settings.
- **Looking up a term?** Check the [Glossary](glossary.md) for definitions of products, regulations, and concepts.
- **Planning an FSI-specific deployment?** Review the [FSI Configuration Examples](fsi-configuration-examples.md) for scenarios matching your institution type.
